📚 Rajasthan Government Exam Information

RPSC (Rajasthan Public Service Commission)

  • RAS Prelims: 200 questions, 1 mark each, 0.33 negative marking
  • RAS Mains: No negative marking
  • SI Exam: 200 questions, 1 mark each, 0.25 negative marking
  • School Lecturer: 300 questions, 1 mark each, 0.33 negative marking

RSMSSB (Rajasthan Staff Selection Board)

  • Junior Assistant: 200 questions, 1 mark each, 0.25 negative marking
  • Lab Assistant: 200 questions, 1 mark each, 0.25 negative marking
  • Stenographer: 200 questions, 1 mark each, 0.25 negative marking

Rajasthan Police

  • Constable Exam: 150 questions, 2 marks each, 0.5 negative marking
  • SI Exam: 200 questions, 1 mark each, 0.25 negative marking

Tips for Rajasthan Exams

  • Understand negative marking pattern before attempting
  • Focus on accuracy over speed
  • Skip questions you're unsure about
  • Practice with previous year papers
